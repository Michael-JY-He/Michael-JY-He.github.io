About

I am a Computer Science PhD student at the University of Texas at Austin, under the guidance of Professor Keshav Pingali.

My research interests include parallel computing on multicore CPUs and GPUs, graph analytics and placement&routing in EDA. In general, I am interested in accelaration by parallelization.

I recevied my B.E. in Electrical Engineering and a second Bachelor in Economics from Tsinghua University in 2014.
