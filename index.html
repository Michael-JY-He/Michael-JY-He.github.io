I'm Michael Jiayuan He!

I'm a computer science PhD student at UT Austin. I'm interested in parallel computing, GPU computing, graph analytics and place&route in EDA. I work under the guidance of Professor Keshav Pingali in the Intelligent Software System (ISS) group. I received my B.E. in Electrical Engineering and a second B.e. in Economics from Tsinghua University in 2014.
